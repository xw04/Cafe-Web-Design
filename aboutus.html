About us

Cafe History

Energy Cafe isn't just a coffee shop — it's a journey back in time to the golden era of American vintage charm. Our story began in 1985, inspired by the bustling diners and vibrant energy of mid-century America. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll be transported to a bygone era, where every sip of our rich, flavorful coffee ignites your spirit and fuels your day.

Our founders, Thomas Dee, envisioned a place where people could escape the hustle and bustle of modern life and indulge in the simple pleasures of great coffee and good company.

Our Mission

Our journey began with a simple mission: to redefine the way you enjoy your daily indulgences. Whether you're craving artisanal coffee blends, delectable pastries, or gourmet meals, our online website has got you covered. We've scoured the globe to source the finest ingredients and products, ensuring that every item in our collection is a testament to quality and taste.

Mission
Energize your day with exceptional coffee. Create a welcoming haven inspired by American vintage charm. Foster connections and camaraderie through hospitality and warmth.
Vision
To be the go-to destination for coffee lovers and vintage enthusiasts. Provide a nostalgic escape where quality and community intertwine. Transport guests to a simpler time, filled with genuine connections and memorable experiences.

The Atmosphere

Step into Energy Cafe, and you'll feel like you've stepped back in time. Our cozy interior is adorned with retro furnishings, vintage posters, and classic memorabilia, creating an ambiance that's as inviting as it is nostalgic. Pull up a seat at our retro counter or sink into one of our plush booths — no matter where you sit, you'll be surrounded by the warmth and comfort of a bygone era.

The Coffee

At Energy Cafe, we take our coffee seriously. Our expert baristas handcraft each cup with care, using only the finest beans sourced from around the world. From bold and robust to smooth and creamy, our carefully curated selection of coffee blends has something to satisfy every palate. And with our vintage-style espresso machines, you can trust that every shot is pulled to perfection, ensuring a consistently delicious brew every time.
